# 🎀 Hello Kitty Study Timer: Animated Workflow 🎀

Welcome to the cutest, coziest study timer workflow! This document outlines the user journey, layout features, and adorable animations that will make studying a stress-free experience.

---

## 🌸 1. The Welcome & Setup (Initial Load)
* **Visuals:** The screen opens with a Soft Pink background that gently fades into a Light Cream (#FFF5F7) canvas. 
* **Animations:** 
  - The "Hello Kitty Study Timer" title playfully drops in from the top with a bouncy spring effect.
  - A small 2D/3D Hello Kitty mascot peeks up from the bottom of the screen, blinks twice, and waves her paw (duration: 600ms).
  - The main timer (set to 25:00) fades in, softly scaling up from 0 to 100%.
  - Tiny pastel stars gently twinkle in the background.

## 🎀 2. Starting a Study Session
* **User Action:** The user clicks the [ Start ] button.
* **Button Animation:** The pill-shaped button visually squishes down completely on click, releasing a soft "pop" ring of color around it. 
* **State Change:** 
  - The background adopts a subtle Pastel Red tint (#FF6B81) to indicate focus mode.
  - Hello Kitty pulls out a little notebook and puts on some reading glasses.
* **Ongoing Animation (Study Mode):**
  - The colon in the timer gently pulses (opacity fading in and out).
  - A soft "progress ring" traces around the edges of the center card.
  - Hello Kitty occasionally taps her pencil or turns a page every few minutes.

## ✨ 3. Pausing the Timer
* **User Action:** The user clicks the [ Pause ] button.
* **State Change:** 
  - The timer text grays out slightly to Soft Gray (#F2F2F2).
  - Hello Kitty puts her pencil down and looks up, waiting patiently.
  - The progress ring pauses and glows softly to remind the user to return soon.

## 🍰 4. Completing a Study Session (Transition to Short Break)
* **Trigger:** The 25:00 timer reaches 00:00.
* **Celebration Animation:** 
  - A shower of tiny ribbon bows and confetti hearts explodes from the center card!
  - A cute popup balloon appears: "Yay! Great job studying! 💖"
* **State Change:**
  - One "Session Strawberry" (or flower) lights up at the bottom screen tracker to mark 1 completed session.
  - The timer auto-updates to 05:00.
  - The background shifts back to a super soft, calming Light Cream.

## 🍵 5. Short Break Mode (5 mins)
* **Visuals:** The screen feels completely relaxed. 
* **Animations:**
  - Hello Kitty takes out a steaming cup of tea. A tiny wisp of steam floats up continuously.
  - Soft, dreamy Lo-Fi music UI expands (if music feature is enabled), pulsating to a slow heartbeat rhythm.
  - The timer numbers sway very gently side to side, creating a calming, "breathing" effect.
* **End of Break:** A soft chime plays, and a popup says "Time to focus again! You can do it! 🌟" 

## 💤 6. The Long Break (After 4 Sessions)
* **Trigger:** The 4th Session Strawberry lights up.
* **Mega Celebration:** Extra confetti, and the background shifts to a lovely Twilight Purple or Dark Pastel theme for ultimate rest.
* **Animations:**
  - The timer sets to 15:00.
  - Hello Kitty changes into pajamas, and a little 'Zzz' animation floats up from her head. 
  - Gently falling pastel shooting stars trace across the background.
* **End of Break:** The sun "rises" in the UI, shifting the colors back to the morning Soft Pink, resetting the tracker to 0.

---

### 🎨 Additional Cute Interactions:
- **Hover States:** Whenever you hover over the [Start], [Pause], or [Reset] buttons, a tiny Hello Kitty bow cursor replaces the default mouse, and the button glows with a white drop-shadow.
- **Streak Tracker:** Collecting 4 Strawberries unlocks a cute accessory for the mascot to wear during the next session!
